🌿 Peace Hub
Peace Hub is a minimalist mental wellness web application designed to help users recalibrate, manage stress, and express their thoughts in a supportive digital environment. Built with a focus on simplicity and accessibility, it provides interactive tools that encourage mindfulness and healthy digital habits.

Live Demo | Report Bug

✨ Features
Peace Hub combines several mindfulness modules into one cohesive experience:

🌬️ Breathing Exercise: A guided, rhythmic animation to help users synchronize their breath and lower cortisol levels.

🫙 Worry Jar: A symbolic space to externalize anxieties. Type your worries and "release" them to clear your mind.

📓 Journal Vault: A private reflection space. Your thoughts are saved securely using browser Local Storage, ensuring privacy without needing a backend.

🫧 Bubble Pop Game: A satisfying, low-stakes mini-game designed for sensory stress relief.

⏳ Digital Detox Timer: A customizable countdown to encourage intentional breaks from screen time.

📊 Wellness Insights: Visualize your self-care journey with simple activity statistics based on your usage patterns.
